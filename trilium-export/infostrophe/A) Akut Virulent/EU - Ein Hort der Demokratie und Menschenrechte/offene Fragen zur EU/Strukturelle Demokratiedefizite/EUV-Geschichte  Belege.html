Wikipedia (Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe):
"However, the rejection of the document by French and Dutch voters in May and June 2005 brought the ratification process to an end. Following a period of reflection, the Treaty of Lisbon was created to replace the Constitutional Treaty. This contained many of the changes that were originally placed in the Constitutional Treaty but, instead of repealing and replacing the existing treaties, simply amended them."

Quelle: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_establishing_a_Constitution_for_Europe

 

Giscard d'Estaing selbst bestätigt Inhaltsgleichheit von EUV und Lissabon-Vertrag:
Valéry Giscard d'Estaing (Autor der EU-Verfassung) in Le Monde (26.10.2007): "les propositions institutionnelles du traité constitutionnel [...] se retrouvent intégralement dans le traité de Lisbonne, mais dans un ordre différent" 

Übersetzung: "Die institutionellen Vorschläge des Verfassungsvertrags finden sich vollständig im Lissabon-Vertrag wieder, nur in anderer Reihenfolge" 

Englisch (The Independent, 30.10.2007): "The EU Treaty is the same as the Constitution" 

Quelle: Zitiert in Wikipedia (Irish European Constitution referendum) https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irish_European_Constitution_referendum

3. Irland: Erst Nein (2008), dann nochmal abstimmen (2009)

Kompakteste Quelle:

House of Commons Library (UK-Parlament):
"In a referendum on 2 October 2009 the Republic of Ireland voted by 67.1% to 32.9% in favour of ratifying the Treaty of Lisbon, having voted against its ratification in June 2008."
Ergebnisse:
- 12. Juni 2008: 53.4% Nein, 46.6% Ja
- 2. Oktober 2009: 67.1% Ja, 32.9% Nein
Quelle: https://commonslibrary.parliament.uk/research-briefings/rp09-75/

 

Zugeständnisse vor 2. Referendum:

"Politically, the second vote was predicated on concessions agreed by the European Council in December 2008 and confirmed in June 2009 as 'guarantees' to be formalised in a protocol attached to the next accession treaty."
Quelle: w.o. (House of Commons Library)
