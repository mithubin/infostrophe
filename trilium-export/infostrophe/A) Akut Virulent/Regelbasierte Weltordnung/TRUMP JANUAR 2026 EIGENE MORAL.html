TRUMP JANUAR 2026: "EIGENE MORAL" ÜBER VÖLKERRECHT

DIE ORIGINALZITATE (8. Januar 2026):

Reporter: "Do you see any checks on your power on the world stage? Is there anything that could stop you if you wanted to?"

Trump: "Yeah, there is one thing. My own morality. My own mind. It's the only thing that can stop me."

Trump: "I don't need international law. I'm not looking to hurt people."

Reporter: "Do you feel your administration needs to abide by international law?"

Trump: "Yeah I do. I do, but it depends what your definition of international law is."
